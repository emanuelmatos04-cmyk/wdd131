Discover DR

Dominican Republic Tourist Guide — Site Plan

Site Name

Discover DR — A tourist guide dedicated to showcasing the culture, beaches, and attractions of the Dominican Republic. The name is short, memorable, and communicates the purpose clearly.

Optional domain: discoverdr.org

Site Purpose

The site provides a hub for travelers interested in visiting the Dominican Republic. It offers information about popular destinations, cultural highlights, local cuisine, and practical travel tips. Visitors can explore guides to Santo Domingo, Punta Cana, Puerto Plata, and other regions, with recommendations for activities, accommodations, and transportation.

Scenarios

  • What are the top beaches to visit in Punta Cana?
  • Where can I find authentic Dominican food in Santo Domingo?
  • What cultural festivals are happening during my travel dates?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #006994 (Caribbean sea blue — used for headers, navigation, and footer)

Accent Color: #f4a300 (Tropical sun orange — used for buttons and highlights)

Background Color: #e9e9e9 (used for body background)

Text Color: #222 (used for body text)

Typography

Headings: Merriweather — elegant and readable for titles.

Body Text: Roboto — clean and modern for paragraphs.
